Top 5 Best Albums

To pimp a butterfly by kendrick lamar is poetry in the music form tracks give off emotion and tone that gives the skin and bones of the tracks while instrumentals and vocals provide the soul
Blkswn by smino starts with slow and soft beats that follow eachother the lyrics are witty and every track is almost a conversation with the next
  Care for me by saba is almost a diary of the artist you can connect with the artist through the storytelling and feeling put into the tracks
  MM...FOOD by mf doom combines 1980's cartoons with rap/hip-hop beats that gives a playfully creative feeling to the album that you rarely see
It is what it is by thundercat is a mellow melodic album with funkadelic and almost space like tones it pairs really well with the bass the artist is known for you can feel the creative expression the artist was looking for
